Privacy Policy

In Office Days is designed with your privacy as the top priority. All attendance and location data is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to any server or cloud. The app does not require an account, does not collect personal information, and does not share your data with anyone.

  • No accounts: You are never asked to sign up or log in.
  • No cloud sync: Your data never leaves your device.
  • No tracking: The app does not track you outside your chosen office location.
  • No ads or analytics: There are no third-party trackers, ads, or analytics SDKs.
  • Full control: You can delete your data at any time from the app settings.

If you have any questions about privacy, please contact support.
